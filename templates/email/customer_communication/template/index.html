December 19, 2021

Dear Valued Customer,

 

I’m pleased to share exciting news from Omnicell as we continue to build the intelligent infrastructure to support your path to the fully autonomous pharmacy. Earlier today, we announced our intent to acquire ReCept Holdings, Inc., a specialty pharmacy managed services organization that will add solutions for specialty pharmacy expertise, operational capabilities, workflow management, payor access assistance, and other aspects of managing a specialty pharmacy to Omnicell’s broader medication management portfolio.

 

This total solution for provider groups, federally qualified health centers (FQHCs), and hospitals supports onsite management of specialty pharmacy services, including payor contracting, staffing, licensing, quality assurance, 340B program administration, and preferred pricing agreements designed to improve margin and profitability, while keeping the patient at the center of care.

 

Specialty pharmacy is an important and complex component of a comprehensive medication management strategy that requires unique capabilities to drive value and growth. The addition of these specialized management services to Omnicell’s intelligent medication management infrastructure is expected to create synergies with offerings like 340B program management and our EnlivenHealth patient engagement solutions that will allow us to serve as a comprehensive strategic partner to help transform your pharmacy care delivery model.

 

We are excited to welcome the ReCept team into the Omnicell organization as they bring unique expertise and talents that will drive continued innovation and support for navigating the increasing complexities of managing a specialty pharmacy.

 

Today's press release provides additional details on the acquisition, which requires regulatory approval and is expected to close by the end of the year. We will update you as developments occur. For further updates, you may also visit our website www.omnicell.com.
